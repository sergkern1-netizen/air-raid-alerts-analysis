## 📖 WHAT IS THIS PROJECT?

This is a research study about **air raid alerts in Ukraine** using data and numbers.

🎯 **In simple terms:**
- We collected information about all air raid alerts that occurred in Ukraine from 2022 to 2026
- We analyzed this data using a computer
- We found interesting patterns and trends
- We can predict when the next alert might happen

## 🔍 WHAT INTERESTING THINGS DID WE FIND?

### 1️⃣ ESCALATION (Threat is growing)

```
2022: ~49 alerts per day
2023: ~95 alerts per day (double!)
2024: ~143 alerts per day
2025: ~252 alerts per day (5x more than 2022!)
```

**What it means:** The situation with air raid alerts has gotten much worse in the last 2 years.

### 2️⃣ MOST DANGEROUS REGIONS

Top 3 most dangerous places:
1. **Dnipropetrovska Region** — most alerts (42,252)
2. **Kharkivska Region** — second place (35,325)
3. **Donetska Region** — third place (26,735)

**What it means:** Three regions have almost half of all alerts. They need more shelters.

### 3️⃣ WESTWARD ESCALATION (Breaking news!)

In the last 6 months, alerts have **dramatically increased in Western Ukraine:**
- **Lvivska Region** — 3.4x more!
- **Ivano-Frankivsk** — 3.4x more!
- **Zakarpattia** — 3.3x more!

**What it means:** The threat is spreading westward. Western cities have become more dangerous.

### 4️⃣ SUSTAINED STRESS

- **Average duration:** ~3-4 hours of alerts per day
- **Worst day:** 14+ hours without break (May 12, 2024)
- **Number of days:** 1,563 consecutive days with alerts

**What it means:** This is constant stress for the population. People live in a state of tension all this time.

### 5️⃣ SEASONAL PATTERNS

```
Spring (March-May):     MOST DANGEROUS
Summer (July-Sept):     Moderate
Fall-Winter:            But never fully safe
```

**What it means:** Be more careful in spring. But the threat never completely disappears.
